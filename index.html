About Us

TrioSpec is a startup company that was incubated at the Cortex Hub in East London, Eastern Cape in South Africa by two former Walter Sisulu University and Nelson Mandela Metropolitan University students.

The goal of the company is to build world-class solutions for the challenges that people face on a daily basis. We specialize in software development but also focusing on any ground-breaking technologies.

We developed shofar mainly because South African cab drivers make living from their cars now tech and large cab companies are making use of technology to blow the self employed drivers under the water. Our app is for all the drivers that still want to be self-employed and passengers that are looking for reasonable cab fare.
